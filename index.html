How to Use

When you launch the MPIPP database for the first time, you will be greeted by a welcome screen. Here you can select to login as either the employer or the participant. In the event that database functionality is impaired, please use the contact us button to schedule an in-person appointment so we can serve your needs appropriately.

Participant Management

Here you can login as the participant and add beneficiaries, view your benefits, edit your profile, etc.

Learn more

Employer Management

Here you can login as the employer and manage participants, view their benefits, create reports, etc.

Learn more

The report creation tool allows you to create easy-to-read reports of contributions and detail sheets and customize what data you wish to include.
